Research

Your patient data can be used in research to improve treatment for serious illnesses and conditions. This information is being used find cures for diseases like diabetes and cancer. Without this data research, the NHS are unable to develop our understanding or improve our services for the future.

Planning

Patient data can also be used to manage and prepare health and social care services in England. With this information the NHS can plan where to place essential services, like heart units and specialist care wards.
